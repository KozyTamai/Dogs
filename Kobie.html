Kobie

Kobie was born on October 10, 2014 and taken in by a family of four a week later.

He loved eating everything he could get his hands on. Whether it was a tree branch, rock or pebble, Kobie ate it regardless. Kobie's favorite treats were the dog bacon strips and meat.

Unfortunately, Kobie passed on January 1, 2019 in his sleep due to kidney failure.
